Penguin Tycoon

An incremental game made during my second year of university. I made this game while learning C++ within Unreal Engine.

Team Size: 1
Time Frame: 5 Months
Engine: Unreal Engine 5.4.4

About

An incremental game about spawning penguins. This game follows the simple loop of buying penguin generators, collecting the penguins they generate, selling those penguins for money, and then buying more items, such as conveyor belts, auto sellers, and more generators! You can also play a fishing mini game to earn fishing points, which can be used to upgrade generators.

Penguin Tycoon is my take on the incremental genre. I've always enjoyed these kinds of games, such as Cookie Clicker, Adventure Capitalist, and Digseum, but I wanted to put a unique spin on the genre by making a 3D game, which isn't often seen in incrementals.

I created this game during my second year of University for one of my assignments. The main objective of the project was to make a game using C++ within Unreal Engine, as that's what we were learning at the time. I used C++ to handle logic such as penguin spawning, collision handling, and saving, but I still used Blueprints for features such as UI.
