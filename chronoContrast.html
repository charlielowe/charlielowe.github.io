Chrono Contrast

Created during the Ukie Student Game Jam 2025. Chrono Contrast is a short puzzle game where you have to progress through a military facility by utilising the power of time travel.

Team Size: 5
Time Frame: 3 Days
Engine: Unreal Engine 5.4.4

About

The theme of the jam was 'contrast'. We implemented this theme by exploring the contrasting differences between present and future, and we challenge the player to utilise these differences in creative ways to solve puzzles. We also integrated contrast in our game through the use of visuals and audio.

My team was made up of 5 games development students. My main roles were programming, asset production, and level design. I created the second level in the game, where players have to bypass enemy AIs by jumping across obstacles. The objects placed around the level in the present are too far apart for players to jump between them, but in the future the objects have changed and moved. By switching between the present and future at the correct moments, players can reach the window at the end of the level.

This project was a great learning experience, and it taught me a lot about making a game as a team, working to a strict deadline, and taking on other roles besides programming.
