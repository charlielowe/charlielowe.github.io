Detective KevPink

A game made during the 2024 Pink Platformer game jam.

Team Size: 2
Time Frame: 2 Weeks
Engine: Godot 4.3

About

Detective KevPink is a game I made with one of my university class mates during the 2024 Pink Platformer game jam. The game jam rules specified that the game had to be a platformer, it had to include the colour pink, and it had to follow the theme "password-protected".

My teammate and I came up with the idea for a platformer where the player has to collect letters throughout the level and then try and spell out a word using those letters at the end of the level.

I was responsible for both programming and creating music for the game, whereas my teammate did programming and art.
